## Platform Overview

### What is EchoDeed?
EchoDeed is a comprehensive K-12 character education platform designed to foster kindness, track community service hours, and engage families through meaningful activities. The platform combines social-emotional learning (SEL) with Christian values, local community partnerships, and real-world rewards.

### Core Philosophy
- **Safety First**: COPPA compliant with AI-powered crisis detection
- **Family Engagement**: Dual reward system involving students and parents
- **Community Connection**: Local Burlington businesses providing real rewards
- **Character Development**: Aligned with SEL standards and Christian values
- **Measurable Impact**: Complete tracking of service hours, participation, and growth

### Platform Access
- **Website**: www.echodeed.com
- **Mobile App**: Progressive Web App (PWA) - installable on any device
- **Demo Access**: One-click role switching for testing
- **Live Status**: Fully operational with 17 Burlington business partners

1. **Service Name** (Required)

3. **Organization Name** (Optional)
   - Name of organization served
   - Example: "Burlington Food Bank"

4. **Contact Information** (Optional for verification)
   - Contact person name
   - Email address
   - Phone number

5. **Hours Logged** (Required)
   - Number input with 0.5 hour increments
   - Minimum: 0.5 hours
   - Maximum: 24 hours per entry
   - Slider or number input

6. **Service Date** (Required)
   - Date picker calendar
   - Cannot be future date
   - Example: "09/25/2025"

7. **Location** (Optional)
   - Where service was performed
   - Example: "Burlington, NC"

   - Dropdown selection:

     - Healthcare & Medicine
     - Community Development
     - Senior Care
     - Animal Welfare
     - Homeless & Food Services
     - Youth Programs
     - Religious Organizations
     - Arts & Culture
     - Special Events
     - Other

   - Minimum 20 characters

10. **Photo Evidence** (Optional)
    - Upload button for photo
    - Helps with verification
    - Not required but recommended

1. Student fills out complete form
2. Clicks "Submit for Verification"
3. Entry goes to "pending" status
4. Teacher receives notification
5. Teacher reviews and approves/rejects
6. Student receives notification of decision
7. If approved, hours added to "verified" total
8. Tokens automatically awarded (5 per hour)
9. Parent notified of approval
