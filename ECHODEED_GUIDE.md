## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How is this different from our existing grade/discipline app?**  
A: Those apps track problems and performance. EchoDeed celebrates CHARACTER and builds belonging through positive recognition. It's complementary, not competitive.

**Q: Do we need to buy new hardware?**  
A: No. EchoDeed works on any smartphone, tablet, or computer with internet access. Students use their own devices.

**Q: What if we already use another system for service hours?**  
A: EchoDeed exports to x2vol and CSV format. It integrates with your existing workflow while adding photo verification for faster approvals.

**Q: How do you handle student privacy?**  
A: All posts are anonymous. No personal data is exposed in the feed. COPPA and FERPA compliant. Parents only see their own child's notifications.

**Q: What about crisis situations or concerning posts?**  
A: Content is moderated through a human review queue. Teachers make all decisions. NO automatic counselor alerts. System is designed to AVOID legal liability by requiring human intervention.

**Q: How much does it cost?**  
A: Contact for custom pricing based on school size. Sponsor partnerships offset costs. Free pilot available for qualifying schools.

**Q: Can parents opt out?**  
A: Yes. Parents control notification preferences. Students can still participate even if parents opt out.

**Q: What if students post inappropriate content?**  
A: Three-layer filtering: (1) Pre-posting profanity filter, (2) AI pattern detection, (3) Teacher moderation queue. Students are educated on appropriate use.
