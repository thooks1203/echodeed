*Protecting Business and Customers While Enabling Innovation*

## **AI PREDICTIONS DISCLAIMER & LIABILITY PROTECTION**

### **Core Principle: Decision Support, Not Medical Diagnosis**

**EchoDeed AI provides wellness INSIGHTS, not medical diagnoses or definitive predictions about individual health outcomes.**

### **Specific Legal Protections**

#### **What Our AI Does:**
✅ Analyzes anonymous behavioral patterns for wellness insights  
✅ Provides statistical risk assessments based on aggregated data  
✅ Suggests interventions and wellness activities  
✅ Identifies trends that may warrant attention  
✅ Supports human decision-making with data-driven insights  

#### **What Our AI Does NOT Do:**
❌ Diagnose mental health conditions or medical issues  
❌ Provide medical advice or treatment recommendations  
❌ Replace professional mental health or medical consultation  
❌ Guarantee prevention of any specific outcome  
❌ Make definitive predictions about individual behavior  

### **Accuracy Standards & Expectations**

**Performance Benchmarks:**
- **Wellness Trend Detection**: 78-87% accuracy rate
- **Risk Pattern Recognition**: 70-85% confidence levels
- **Intervention Suggestions**: Based on peer-reviewed research
- **False Positive Management**: <15% false alert rate target

**Continuous Improvement Protocol:**
- Quarterly accuracy audits and model retraining
- Feedback loop integration from real-world outcomes
- Third-party validation of algorithmic performance
- Transparent reporting of accuracy metrics to customers

## **CUSTOMER PROTECTION PROTOCOLS**

### **Required Human Oversight Framework**

#### **For K-8 Schools:**
- **Teacher Review Required**: All student wellness alerts reviewed by certified educators
- **Parent Notification**: Parents notified of any wellness concerns with clear context
- **Professional Referral**: School counselors involved in any high-risk situations
- **Documentation**: Clear records of AI insights vs. human decisions

#### **For Corporate Customers:**
- **Manager Training**: All supervisors trained on AI insight interpretation
- **HR Oversight**: Human Resources involved in any employee wellness concerns
- **Professional Resources**: Clear pathways to Employee Assistance Programs (EAP)
- **Privacy Protection**: Individual identity never disclosed in AI reports

**High-Risk Alert Procedure:**
1. **Immediate Human Review**: Alert triggers human professional review within 4 hours
2. **Multiple Validation**: Cross-reference with other behavioral indicators
3. **Professional Consultation**: Mental health professional confirms intervention need
4. **Documented Decision Trail**: Clear record of AI input vs. human judgment
5. **Follow-up Protocol**: Track outcomes to improve future accuracy
