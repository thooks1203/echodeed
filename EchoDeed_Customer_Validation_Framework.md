# EchoDeed™ Customer Validation Framework
*Validating Real Demand & Pricing Before Scale*

---

## **CRITICAL VALIDATION REQUIREMENTS**

### **Problem: Assumption-Based Pricing**
**Current Risk**: Business plan shows $35K/school pricing with no validation from actual budget holders
**Solution**: Systematic validation with 20+ decision-makers before any major investment

---

## **K-8 SCHOOL CUSTOMER VALIDATION PROTOCOL**

### **Phase 1: Problem Validation (Weeks 1-2)**

#### **Target Interviewees (15 minimum):**
- **5 Private School Administrators** (Primary decision makers)  
- **3 Public School Principals** (Budget influence)
- **4 School District Technology Directors** (Procurement process)
- **3 School Counselors/SEL Coordinators** (End user needs)

#### **Key Validation Questions:**

**Problem Discovery:**
1. "What's your biggest challenge with student wellness/SEL tracking?"
2. "How much time does your staff spend on wellness documentation weekly?"
3. "What tools do you currently use for SEL compliance?"
4. "What would happen if you could predict student wellness issues 2-4 weeks early?"

**Budget Reality Check:**
5. "What do you currently spend annually on SEL/wellness programs?"
6. "Who makes decisions about spending $25K+ on educational technology?"
7. "What's your typical procurement timeline for new platforms?"
8. "What would need to be true for you to allocate $35K to a wellness platform?"

### **Phase 2: Solution Validation (Weeks 3-4)**

#### **Demo Testing Protocol:**
- **Live Platform Demo**: Show actual AI features to 10+ educators
- **Feature Priority Ranking**: What matters most vs. least
- **Usability Testing**: Can teachers actually use this effectively?
- **Privacy Comfort Level**: Are administrators comfortable with AI wellness monitoring?

#### **Pricing Validation Framework:**

**Van Westendorp Price Sensitivity Analysis:**
1. "At what price would this platform be too expensive?"
2. "At what price would you question the platform's quality?"  
3. "At what price would this platform be a bargain?"
4. "At what price would you definitely purchase this platform?"

**Expected Results Validation:**
- **Acceptable Range**: $15K-45K annually
- **Optimal Price Point**: $25K-35K annually  
- **Deal Breakers**: >$50K annually
- **Budget Timeline**: 6-18 month procurement cycle

### **Phase 3: Purchase Intent Validation (Weeks 5-6)**

#### **Letter of Intent (LOI) Collection:**
**Target**: 5+ signed Letters of Intent from qualified schools

**LOI Requirements:**
- Specific budget allocation timeline
- Decision-maker signature
- Pilot program commitment
- Success metrics agreement

**Qualification Criteria:**
- 200+ students (adequate scale for platform value)
- Existing SEL budget >$20K annually  
- Decision authority within 90 days
- Willingness to be reference customer

## **CORPORATE CUSTOMER VALIDATION PROTOCOL**

### **Phase 1: Market Research (Weeks 1-3)**

#### **Target Interviewees (20 minimum):**
- **8 CHROs/VP HR** (Budget authority)
- **6 Wellness Program Managers** (End user influence)
- **4 Employee Experience Directors** (Strategic alignment)
- **2 Benefits/Insurance Managers** (Cost justification)

#### **Corporate Budget Reality Check:**

**Key Questions:**
1. "What do you currently spend on employee wellness programs annually?"
2. "How do you measure ROI on wellness investments?"
3. "What would anonymous burnout prediction be worth to prevent one resignation?"
4. "Who approves wellness technology purchases >$50K annually?"

**Expected Budget Validation:**
- **Small Companies (100-500 employees)**: $2-5K monthly ($24-60K annually)
- **Mid-Market (500-2000 employees)**: $8-15K monthly ($96-180K annually)
- **Enterprise (2000+ employees)**: $15-50K monthly ($180-600K annually)

### **Phase 2: Pilot Program Validation**

#### **Corporate Pilot Requirements:**
- **Duration**: 90-120 day minimum
- **Success Metrics**: Defined upfront with measurable KPIs
- **Stakeholder Commitment**: Executive sponsor + implementation team
- **Expansion Timeline**: Clear path from pilot to full deployment

**Pilot Success Metrics:**
- **Participation Rate**: >60% employee engagement
- **Accuracy Validation**: AI predictions align with manager observations
- **ROI Demonstration**: Measurable improvement in wellness metrics
- **User Satisfaction**: >75% positive feedback from participants

## **VALIDATION SUCCESS CRITERIA**

### **Minimum Validation Requirements BEFORE Scaling:**

#### **K-8 Schools:**
✅ **15+ Problem Validation Interviews** (completed)  
✅ **5+ Solution Demos** with positive feedback  
✅ **3+ Letters of Intent** from qualified schools  
✅ **Price Validation**: 70%+ find pricing acceptable  
✅ **Timeline Validation**: Clear 6-18 month procurement understanding

#### **Corporate:**
✅ **20+ Problem Validation Interviews** (completed)  
✅ **2+ Pilot Programs** initiated with enterprise customers  
✅ **ROI Framework** validated with finance/HR stakeholders  
✅ **Competitive Analysis** confirms pricing advantage  
✅ **Decision-Maker Access** established for key prospects

### **Go/No-Go Decision Framework**

**GREEN LIGHT** (Proceed with confidence):
- 80%+ of interviewees confirm problem urgency
- 60%+ find proposed pricing reasonable  
- 3+ Letters of Intent collected
- Clear competitive advantage validated
- Procurement process understood

**YELLOW LIGHT** (Pivot pricing/positioning):
- 60-80% problem validation
- Pricing resistance >40%
- Feature gaps identified
- Longer sales cycles than expected
- Competitive threats emerging

**RED LIGHT** (Major pivot required):
- <60% problem validation
- Pricing resistance >60%
- No Letters of Intent after 30+ conversations
- Insurmountable competitive disadvantages
- Legal/regulatory barriers identified
