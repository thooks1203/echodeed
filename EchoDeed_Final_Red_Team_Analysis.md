**What Happens:**

- Social media campaign: "They're using AI to spy on our children"

**Probability**: 25-35%  
**Impact**: Business failure

### **Scenario B: "The Technical Failure"**

**What Happens:**

- Teacher makes intervention based on AI recommendation
- Parents blame platform for "false diagnosis"

**Probability**: 40-50%  
**Impact**: Legal and PR crisis  
**Prevention**: Lower accuracy claims, require human validation, liability insurance

**What Happens:**

**Probability**: 60-70% within 18 months  
**Impact**: Market elimination  
**Prevention**: Speed to market, exclusive partnerships, differentiated features

### **Scenario D: "The Economic Downturn"**

**What Happens:**
- Economic recession hits education and corporate budgets
- "Wellness software" seen as luxury expense
- Schools cancel subscriptions to preserve core teaching functions
- Corporate customers freeze wellness spending

**Probability**: 30-40% in next 2 years  
**Impact**: Revenue collapse  
**Prevention**: Essential positioning, recession-proof value proposition, lower pricing tiers

### **Scenario E: "The Regulatory Shutdown"**

**What Happens:**

- FERPA interpretation changes regarding AI analysis of student data
