### **FAQ Framework for Parents**

#### **Q: "Is this spying on my child?"**
**A: "Not at all. It's like having a caring teacher notice patterns - but anonymous and focused only on wellbeing. Your child chooses what to share, and it's about kindness, not surveillance."**

#### **Q: "What if the AI is wrong about my child?"**  
**A: "AI only suggests - never decides. Every alert goes to trained teachers who know your child personally. Think of it as a 'heads up' tool, not a judgment system."**

#### **Q: "Who sees my child's data?"**
**A: "Only your child's teacher and school counselor see wellness patterns. Other students never see this information. Parents get updates about their own child only."**

#### **Q: "Can my child opt out?"**
**A: "Absolutely. Your child can participate as much or as little as they're comfortable with. This is about supporting wellness, not requiring participation."**

#### **Q: "What happens to the data when my child graduates?"**
**A: "All data is permanently deleted within 60 days of graduation or withdrawal. We don't create permanent records that follow your child."**

## **CRISIS COMMUNICATION PROTOCOLS**

### **If Privacy Concerns Arise**

**Immediate Response Protocol:**
1. **Acknowledge concerns** - "We understand privacy is critical"
2. **Explain protections** - Specific technical and policy safeguards  
3. **Offer transparency** - Show exactly what data exists and how it's used
4. **Provide control** - Easy opt-out and data deletion options
5. **Follow up** - Check back to ensure concerns are resolved

### **If Data Incident Occurs**

**Communication Timeline:**
- **Immediate (within 4 hours)**: Contain incident and assess scope
- **24 hours**: Notify affected schools/companies with preliminary details
- **72 hours**: Public communication with full details and remediation plan
- **Ongoing**: Weekly updates until incident fully resolved

**Message Framework:**
*"We take privacy breaches extremely seriously. Here's exactly what happened, what information was affected, what we're doing to fix it, and how we're preventing it from happening again."*
