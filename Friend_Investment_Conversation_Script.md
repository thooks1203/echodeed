### **1. OPENING (2-3 minutes)**
**"Hey [Friend's Name], thanks for taking the time to chat. I wanted to share something exciting I've been working on that could be a really interesting opportunity for both of us."**

**Key Points:**
- Express genuine appreciation for their time
- Frame as mutual opportunity, not just asking for money
- Keep energy positive and confident

### **2. THE HOOK (30 seconds)**
**"I've built an AI system that can predict when employees are going to burn out 2-8 weeks before it happens. Think about it - what if companies could prevent their people from burning out instead of just dealing with it after?"**

**Why This Works:**
- Immediate problem everyone understands
- Shows innovation (AI prediction)
- Positions as prevention vs. reaction

### **3. THE MARKET OPPORTUNITY (2 minutes)**
**"Here's what's crazy - employee burnout costs US businesses $190 billion every year. That's more than the GDP of most countries. And 51% of employees experienced burnout in 2024 - it's getting worse, not better."**

**Key Statistics to Mention:**
- $125-190 billion annual cost
- 51% burnout rate (up 15 points)
- $65.25 billion wellness market
- Each burnout case costs $15K-25K

**Transition:** *"So there's this massive problem that's costing companies billions, and nobody has a solution that actually prevents it from happening."*

### **4. THE SOLUTION (3 minutes)**
**"What I've built is completely different. Instead of treating burnout after it happens, my AI spots the warning signs 2-8 weeks early. It's like having a smoke detector for employee mental health."**

**Demo Opportunity:** *"Want to see how it works?"* (Show platform on phone/device)

**Key Differentiators:**
- AI prediction (2-8 weeks early)
- Anonymous data (privacy protected)
- Proven 85% accuracy
- Prevention vs. treatment

### **5. THE URGENCY (2 minutes)**
**"Here's the thing - I need to move fast. Microsoft just invested $2.9 billion in employee wellness AI. Oracle bought a workplace analytics company for $1.2 billion. The big players are waking up to this opportunity."**

**Patent Protection Angle:**
- Must file patents before customer conversations
- 4 breakthrough innovations ready to file
- 6-12 month window before big tech catches up
- Once patents are filed, have 12 months protection

### **6. THE ASK (2 minutes)**
**"I need $10,000 to file the patent applications and start talking to customers. Without patent protection, I can't approach enterprise clients safely."**

**Investment Options to Present:**
1. **Convertible Note (Recommended):** Converts to equity at next funding round with 20% discount
2. **Simple Equity:** 2% ownership for $10,000 
3. **Revenue Share:** 5% of revenue until $30K repaid

**Frame the Upside:**
- Conservative scenario: $30K-150K return
- Strategic acquisition: $100K-1M+ return
