## Why Parent Notifications Matter

**The Problem:**  
Most parent-school communication is negative:
- "Your child missed homework"  
- "Please sign this discipline form"  
- "Your student is failing math"  

**The Result:**  
Parents dread emails from school. Family conversations about school become stressful. Attendance suffers.

---

**The EchoDeed Solution:**  
What if parents received **positive notifications** every time their teen did something kind at school?

**The Result:**  
- Parents look forward to hearing from school  
- Dinner conversations shift from "How was school?" (grunt) to "Tell me about the kindness you shared today"  
- Families reconnect around positive school experiences  
- Attendance improves because school becomes associated with positive identity
