## ‚ùì **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **Q: Is this replacing traditional counseling services?**
**A:** No, this supplements existing support. EchoDeed‚Ñ¢ focuses on prevention and positive community building. Traditional counseling remains available for students who need individual support.

### **Q: Will other students know what my child posts?**
**A:** No, all posts are completely anonymous. Even school staff cannot identify who wrote specific posts. The focus is on the kindness itself, not who performed it.

### **Q: What if my child doesn't want to participate?**
**A:** Participation is voluntary. Students can benefit from reading others' posts without writing their own. There's no pressure or requirement to post regularly.

### **Q: How do you prevent cyberbullying or negative content?**
**A:** AI filters block inappropriate content automatically. The platform is designed only for positive posts. Any concerning content is immediately flagged for school counselor review.

### **Q: Will this take time away from academics?**
**A:** No, EchoDeed‚Ñ¢ supports academic success by improving school climate and student well-being. Students who feel emotionally supported perform better academically.

### **Q: What data do you collect about my child?**
**A:** We collect minimal data - only what's necessary for the platform to function. No personal information is stored with posts. All data is encrypted and FERPA compliant.

### **Q: Can I see what my child is posting?**
**A:** Posts are anonymous even to parents to encourage authentic sharing. However, you'll receive general reports about participation levels and program outcomes.

### **Q: What if my child encounters someone in crisis?**
**A:** The platform includes crisis detection features that immediately alert school counselors. Students are also taught how to recognize warning signs and report concerns to adults.
