## üéØ **KEY STRATEGIC PRINCIPLES**

### **1. Schools Pay the Bills, Sponsors Add Credibility**
- Schools: 85-90% of revenue (predictable, recurring)
- Sponsors: 10-15% of revenue (supplemental, validates community investment)

### **2. Scale Before Selling to National Brands**
- Don't waste time pitching Target until you have 30,000+ students
- National brands need proof of scale + ROI data
- Focus on school expansion first, national sponsors later

### **3. Use A&T Research Strategically**
- Phase 1-2: "Research in progress with A&T State University"
- Phase 3: "Published findings show X% attendance improvement, Y% behavioral reduction"
- Don't promise research results you don't have yet

### **4. Pricing Philosophy**
- Schools: $6,000-8,000/year (high-margin, core business)
- Local sponsors: $750-1,500 pilots ‚Üí $1,500-3,000/year ongoing
- Regional sponsors: $1,500-5,000/year (CSR budgets)
- National sponsors: $10,000 pilots ‚Üí $50,000-100,000/year (enterprise)

### **5. Conservative Projections, Aggressive Execution**
- This plan assumes 50% success rates (conservative)
- If you hit 75% success rates, revenue could be 1.5x higher
- Focus on execution quality over quantity
