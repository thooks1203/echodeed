## üéØ **CASEL Framework Overview**

### **The Five Core Competencies**
The Collaborative for Academic, Social, and Emotional Learning (CASEL) identifies five interrelated competencies that are the foundation of effective SEL programming:

1. **Self-Awareness** - Understanding emotions, values, and strengths
2. **Self-Management** - Regulating emotions and behaviors effectively
3. **Social Awareness** - Understanding others and developing empathy
4. **Relationship Skills** - Establishing healthy relationships and communication
5. **Responsible Decision-Making** - Making ethical, constructive choices

### **Implementation Settings**
CASEL emphasizes SEL development across four key settings:
- **Classrooms** - Integrated academic and SEL instruction
- **Schools** - School-wide policies and practices
- **Families** - Home-school collaboration
- **Communities** - Community partnerships and engagement
