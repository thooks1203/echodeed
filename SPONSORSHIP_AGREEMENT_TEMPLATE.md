## 1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Sponsor agrees to support the **Student Ambassador Program** at Eastern Guilford High School in Gibsonville, NC, designed to promote character education and community engagement through peer-to-peer recruitment.

**Program Details:**
- **School:** Eastern Guilford High School, Gibsonville, NC
- **Student Count:** 1,200 students (grades 9-12)
- **Ambassador Count:** 40 students (20 Founding Ambassadors + 20 Ambassador Associates)
- **Campaign Duration:** 90 days from program launch
- **Target Recruitment:** 400 students (33% of school population)

## 3. DELIVERABLES

### **EchoDeed Responsibilities:**

1. **Ambassador Recruitment & Training**
   - Select and recruit 40 student ambassadors
   - Provide ambassador orientation and training materials
   - Assign unique referral codes to each ambassador

2. **Referral Tracking System**
   - Build and maintain digital referral tracking dashboard
   - Provide Sponsor with real-time access to campaign metrics
   - Track individual ambassador performance and goal achievement

3. **Reporting & Analytics**
   - Weekly email updates with campaign progress
   - Final comprehensive report at campaign conclusion including:
     - Total students recruited
     - Individual ambassador performance metrics
     - ROI calculation (cost per student acquisition)
     - Student testimonials and photos
     - Recommendations for program expansion

4. **Gift Card Distribution**
   - Coordinate with Sponsor to obtain 20 physical gift cards
   - Distribute cards to qualifying ambassadors who meet recruitment goals
   - Track redemption and provide confirmation to Sponsor

5. **Recognition Ceremony**
   - Organize award ceremony for qualifying ambassadors
   - Provide certificates of achievement for all participants
   - Photograph event and provide images to Sponsor for marketing

### **Sponsor Responsibilities:**

1. **Financial Contribution**
   - Provide $500 total investment within 7 days of Agreement execution
   - Coordinate gift card purchase with local branch/office

2. **Brand Assets (Optional)**
   - Provide logo, brand colors, and marketing materials for campaign promotion
   - Approve use of Sponsor name and logo in ambassador materials

3. **Engagement (Optional)**
   - Attend final award ceremony (if desired)
   - Provide brief remarks to student ambassadors (if desired)
