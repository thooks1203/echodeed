## Terms Details

### Conversion Mechanics:
- **Qualified Financing:** Series A round >$1.5M from institutional investor
- **Automatic Conversion:** All preferred shares convert to common at Series A
- **Discount Application:** 20% discount to Series A price per share
- **Valuation Cap Protection:** If Series A values company >$8M, cap applies

### Liquidation Waterfall:
1. **Series Seed Preferred:** Return of investment plus 8% cumulative dividend
2. **Common Stock:** Remaining proceeds distributed pro rata
3. **Participating Rights:** Non-participating (preferred takes greater of 1x or conversion)

### Anti-Dilution Protection:
- **Weighted Average:** Narrow-based adjustment for stock options and employee grants
- **Protection Events:** Down rounds, recapitalizations, stock splits
- **Carve-Outs:** Standard exceptions for employee equity grants <15% pool
