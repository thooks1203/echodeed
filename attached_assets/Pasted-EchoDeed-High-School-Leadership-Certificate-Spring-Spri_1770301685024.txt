The Spring Sprint is an accelerated leadership track designed to recognize students who demonstrate exceptional character, mentoring, and community impact. This program provides students with a professional credential for college applications and the opportunity to compete for one of two $500 Leadership Scholarships.

üìã The Three Requirements for Completion
1. Mentor Training (The Foundation)
Students must complete the five core online mentor training modules via the EchoDeed platform.
The Goal: Master the fundamentals of active listening, empathy, and student guidance.
The Deliverable: A 250-word personal reflection on how these skills apply to their future career goals.
2. Impact Quests (The Action)
Students must log and verify four acts of community service or kindness.
The Wildcat Rule: At least one of these acts must involve a formal mentoring session with a student from the Middle School pilot.
The Evidence: Each quest requires a photo or a verification signature from a teacher or community supervisor.
3. Leadership Portfolio Defense (The Validation)
Finalists will participate in a 10-minute "Portfolio Defense" with a faculty coach or community panel.
The Goal: Articulate the impact of their service and training.
The Deliverable: A final endorsement signature from the faculty evaluator confirming the student has met the "EchoDeed Standard."

üèÜ The EchoDeed Scholarship Awards
While all students who finish the requirements will receive the Leadership Certificate, the top two performing students will be named EchoDeed Scholars and receive a $500 Scholarship Grant during the end-of-year awards ceremony.
How Winners Are Chosen:
Peer Validation: The volume of unique peer nominations received through "The Echo Award."
Mentorship Depth: High-quality engagement and feedback from the middle school mentoring sessions.
Initiative: Students who organized or led their Kindness Quests rather than just participating.
Defense Scoring: The overall score from the faculty panel during the final Portfolio Defense.
