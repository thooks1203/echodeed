# EchoDeed™ High School
## Features & Benefits Implementation Guide
### Grades 9-12 | 200-Hour Service-Learning Diploma | Advanced Character Education Platform

---

## Executive Summary

EchoDeed™ High School Edition is a comprehensive service-learning management system designed for students in grades 9-12. This deployment features the full 5-phase IPARD workflow, mandatory 200-hour Service-Learning Diploma tracking, advanced verification systems, and achievement-focused rewards including VIP parking passes.

---

## Platform Features

### 1. The 5-Phase IPARD Workflow

The IPARD (Investigation, Preparation, Action, Reflection, Demonstration) model ensures meaningful service learning:

| Phase | Description | Token Bonus |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Investigation** | Research community needs and opportunities | — |
| **Preparation** | Plan service activities and set goals | 25 tokens (after completing I+P) |
| **Action** | Perform verified service hours | Hourly tokens |
| **Reflection** | Journal about experiences and learnings | 50 tokens |
| **Demonstration** | Present/share learning outcomes | 75 tokens |

**Process-Based Motivation:** Students earn bonus tokens (25/50/75) for completing phases, not just logging hours.

### 2. 200-Hour Service-Learning Diploma

**Required Annual Goals:**
- Freshman (9th): 40 hours
- Sophomore (10th): 50 hours
- Junior (11th): 55 hours
- Senior (12th): 55 hours
- **Total: 200 hours for graduation**

**Tracking Features:**
- Real-time progress visualization
- Monthly goal reminders
- Counselor oversight dashboard
- Transcript-ready reports
- Diploma verification system
